About Me

Hi there! Glad to see you here.

Hi! I'm Sateesh Kumar, a passionate Front-End Engineer currently in my 3rd year of B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering at Tula’s Institute, Dehradun. I come from a humble village called Jhakhara, Jagdishpur in Bihar, where my journey into the world of learning and technology began. My early education took place across multiple schools — from King Garden School to Bhartiya Model School (Gahiri) and then Urdu Kanya Vidyalaya in my hometown. I completed my 9th and 10th from Adarsh High School, Jagdishpur, followed by 11th and 12th from Ramlakhan Singh Yadav College. After high school, I dedicated a year to prepare for the JEE exam, securing a 70 percentile. This phase taught me discipline and resilience. Soon after, I moved to Dehradun to pursue my engineering dreams. Today, I'm focused on building beautiful and responsive front-end experiences using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I’m driven by a love for clean UI, user-first design, and a mindset to always keep learning. My roots keep me grounded, and my vision pushes me forward.
